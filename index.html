Brain Zoo

An interactive 3D comparison of brain sizes across the animal kingdom.

Every brain on the turntable is a procedurally generated 3D model, scaled in real time by the metric you pick — absolute brain mass, neuron count, encephalization quotient, or brain-to-body ratio. Switch metrics and watch the brains reshape and re-rank, revealing the twist that bigger isn't always smarter.

Built with Three.js + Vite. Geometry is generated from layered ridged noise (no external models), and all data lives in one editable file.

Data sources: comparative neuroanatomy tables (brain & body masses) and Jerison's encephalization-quotient literature. Neuron counts and EQ are representative, rounded figures.
